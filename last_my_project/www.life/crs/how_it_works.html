How It Works

At LifeDNA, we use an extensive database of peer-reviewed studies to create DNA-personalized health and wellness recommendations to help you live a better life.

DNA Analysis

The first step is to analyze your DNA. We can analyze your DNA data from sites like 23andMe or Ancestry, or send you a DNA kit of our own.

Research Database

Our team of researchers reviews every single study and each single nucleotide polymorphism (SNP) to make sure the results are validated. Since many studies lack the proper research protocols to ensure unbiased results, it’s critical to assess every single study for accuracy and validity.

Personalized Reports

After comparing the SNPs (single nucleotide polymorphisms) in your DNA with our research database, we generate personalized reports that explain your genetic strengths, weaknesses, and potential health concerns and actionable insights to manage your genetic predispositions.

Improve Your Body & Mind

If you’re ready to improve your health, body, and mind like never before, you can join LifeDNA’s Meal & Fitness Program. We create a unique meal plan and workout routines just for you, and it evolves with you to make sure you’re always getting the best possible results.

Some of Our Basis for Determining Scientific Value Include:
  • Research Methods: Did they employ Genome-Wide Association? Is this a meta-analysis?
  • Experimental Design: Samples and population structure, Hardy-Weinberg equilibrium, multiplicity correction, and more.
  • Statistical Results: p value (whether the results are significant), effect size (odds ratio and beta coefficient), and more.

Overall, this creates a database of research you can trust for its accuracy and validity.
